my name is Pilar.



Growing up as a child in Peru, I always found something to keep me occupied. I’d turn my boredom into productivity, organizing my barbie accessories, clothes by color, and toys by category.

My passion for organizing persisted as I got older and complementing it with my love for helping others.

My love for tidiness inspired me to create a Professional Organizing Business.

I look forward to helping you create your dream space, so you can enjoy time with the ones you love, decrease stress, and improve your overall well-being and productivity.
