Moving & Relocation

Overwhelmed by an upcoming move or transfer? Let us help you settle in with a system! We can help you by:

  • Sorting and decluttering prior to your move
  • Inventorying box contents and items to moved and stored
  • Supervising movers and furniture placement on the day of move
  • Coordinating the sale and donation of furniture and other items
  • Carefully packing items to move
  • Preparing a plan for furniture and accessories placement in the new home

My love for tidiness
inspired me to create

Growing up as a child in Peru, I always found something to keep me occupied. I’d turn my boredom into productivity, organizing my barbie accessories, clothes by color, and toys by category. My passion for organizing persisted as I got older and complementing it with my love for helping others.

My love for tidiness inspired me to create a Professional Organizing Business. I look forward to helping you create your dream space, so you can enjoy time with the ones you love, decrease stress, and improve your overall well-being and productivity.
